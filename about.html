About Us

One of the world's leading suppliers of Gum Arabic and natural commodities.

Gezira Hila Impex, founded in 1997, has expanded inside and outside Sudan through company offices and dedicated agents in different regions and states. We have earned a strong reputation by entering global markets and delivering consistently on quality, supply, and service.

Our Journey

Twenty-seven years in the making.

1997

Founded in Khartoum

Eng. Abdulraouf Babikir Saad establishes Gezira Hila Impex with a vision to bring Sudan's finest natural commodities to international markets.

2003

Expansion across Sudan

Network of agents established across the Gum Belt — Kordofan, Darfur, White Nile and Blue Nile States — securing direct access to source.

2010

Entering global markets

First exports to Europe and Asia. Partnerships forged with food and pharmaceutical buyers in Germany, Italy, China and Japan.

2017

Dubai office opens

A regional hub in the UAE bridges Sudanese supply with global demand, streamlining logistics and customer service across continents.

2024

A group of twelve

Today, Gezira Hila Impex stands as a parent to twelve sub-companies trading across diverse product categories and serving four continents.

Our Mission

What we aim for.

To provide high-quality, natural raw materials and deliver competitive services to global markets. Our goal is to always fulfill our markets' needs for quality raw materials — strengthening and sustaining trust in global trade.

We believe in long-term partnerships, in honoring tradition while embracing modern standards, and in elevating Sudan's position in the world's natural commodity supply chain.
