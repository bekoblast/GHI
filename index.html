About Gezira

A Sudanese house with a global reach.

Founded in 1997 by Eng. Abdulraouf Babikir Saad, Gezira Hila Impex grew from a single trading office in Khartoum into one of the world's largest, leading suppliers of Gum Arabic and other natural commodities. Through offices in Dubai and a network of agents across Sudan, our reputation now spans four continents.

We are a family of growers, sorters, cleaners, and exporters — bridging traditional Sudanese harvesting with the standards demanded by modern food, pharmaceutical, and industrial markets.

Sudanese Heritage

Born of the Gum Belt.

Our trade is rooted in a centuries-old Sudanese tradition — acacia tappers, family farms, and the dry-season harvest of the African Gum Belt. The hands that work the trees today carry knowledge passed down for generations.

"Every nodule of gum tells the story of a tree, a tapper, and a season under the Sudanese sun."
